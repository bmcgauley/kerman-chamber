Advocating for Kerman Businesses Since 1950

Since 1950, the Kerman Chamber of Commerce has been the unwavering voice of local business. What began as a small collective of shop owners has grown into a powerful engine for economic development, dedicated to advocating for our members and educating the community on the vital importance of local enterprise.

Our mission is threefold: to advocate for policies that foster growth, to educate emerging leaders through mentorship and resources, and to connect business owners with the partners they need to thrive in a free enterprise system.

We believe that a strong business community is the backbone of a vibrant city. By fostering connections and providing resources, we ensure that Kerman remains a place where innovation is welcomed, and hard work is rewarded.
